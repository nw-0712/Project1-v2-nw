Our Services & Therapies

All our therapies & programs below help you to become healthier mentally & physically.

Biohacking therapy

We support individuals to improve their lifestyle, becoming healthier, accelerate personal growth and cognitive enhancement, by using an advanced approach of combining, innovative methods, therapies, practices, suppliments, experts and cutting-edge technologies.

Through various screening and other DNA testing we work with you to prevent or eradicate fatal genetic diseases and identify genetic risk factors and your deficiencies. Nutrigenomics therapy

In our nutrigenomics service we focus on how you interact with food. We explore how your genes affect your body's response to food.

Immersion Program

This focuses on enhancing your cognitive development and capabilities. It involves deep mental involvement in which you will go through various cognitive processes (with or without sensory stimulation) will cause a shift in your attentional state.

Nootropics Program

Our nootropics program includes a carefully selected combination of supplements, drinks, and foods, that enhance and boost your brains performance.

Neuroplasticity therapy

This service will arm you with the ability to change, improve, grow new connections and adapt over the course of a lifetime with new neural networks, to reprogram your habits, thinking and response to life events.

Rehabilitation & Recovery

This program focuses on working with patients that have experienced extreme trauma. Through a blend of experts, practises, therapies, supplements and products we help accelerate recovery and rehabilitation.
